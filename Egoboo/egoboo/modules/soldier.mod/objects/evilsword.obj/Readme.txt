The Sword of Evil is a magical sword wich has a evil aura
when used. This evil aura does some evil damage to your
opponent. Players with good aligment cannot use this sword.

TIP: You can use the sword to kill speacial monsters weak to
     evil power. (Angle or Sprite for example)
HINT: Do not try to pick up the sword if you have a good 
      aligment...
